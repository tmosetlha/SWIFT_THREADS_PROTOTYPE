How it works

One delivery, start to finish

Seven steps. Two sides of the marketplace, one continuous system — the path every order takes.

  1. 01 Customer

    Request

    Sign up with phone or email and request a delivery — paying by card, EFT, or wallet.

  2. 02 System · AI

    Match

    The engine finds the best available driver, weighing location and workload.

  3. 03 Driver

    Navigate

    Turn-by-turn navigation to the pickup point, then on to drop-off.

  4. 04 Customer

    Track live

    Follow the driver's location on the map in real time — like ride-hailing.

  5. 05 Driver

    Confirm

    Closed out with proof — a photo, PIN code, or signature.

  6. 06 Both sides

    Rate

    Each side rates the experience, feeding future matching and incentives.

  7. 07 Admin

    Oversee

    Every order, rating and payout lands on the admin dashboard — full end-to-end visibility with reporting on performance and delivery times.

Built for every side of the trip

  • Onboarding — sign up with phone or email, verify identity
  • Cart & checkout — card, EFT, or mobile wallet
  • Live order tracking — Uber-style until delivery
  • Ratings & reviews — rate the driver and experience
  • Order history & quick reorder
  • Loyalty points — earn and redeem on purchases & referrals
  • Onboarding & verification — ID, licence, vehicle documents
  • Job requests — clear pickup and drop-off locations
  • Navigation — integrated maps with live directions
  • Proof of delivery — photo, PIN code, or signature
  • Earnings dashboard — per-km + 30% monthly bonus
  • Incentives — priority jobs from high ratings & streaks
  • Dashboard — real-time overview of drivers, customers & orders
  • Order management — track every delivery end-to-end
  • Driver management — approve, track ratings and payments
  • Payments & payouts — handle escrow and releases
  • Analytics — revenue, delivery times, customer trends
  • AI tools — smart matching, fraud detection, predictive ETAs
